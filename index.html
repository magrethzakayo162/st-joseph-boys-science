About The School

St Joseph Boys' Science Secondary School is a government-recognized science-based institution located in Mwanga District, Kilimanjaro Region, Tanzania. The school is committed to academic excellence, discipline, spiritual growth, and leadership development.

We provide high-quality science education combined with moral values, Christian principles, technology integration, and practical learning. Students are prepared for national examinations and future professional careers in medicine, engineering, technology, business, and leadership.

The school promotes holistic development including sports, innovation, teamwork, community service, and spiritual formation through regular prayers and religious activities.
